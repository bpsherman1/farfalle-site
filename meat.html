Grandma's Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef/pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup romano/parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Onion
  • Oregano
  • Red pepper

Instructions

Place beef/pork in a bowl

Add egg, salt, pepper, minced garlic, breadcrumbs, romano cheese, and any other herbs or onion

Mix everything together thoroughly

Form golf ball sized meatballs

Preheat a pan or skillet for a few minutes then add olive oil

Cook meatballs on medium heat until they are brown (about 8 minutes)

Crispy Chicken Cutlets

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Flour
  • 1 egg
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1 cup romano/parmesan cheese

Instructions

Lay your flour, egg, and breadcrumbs out in separate dishes

Take your chicken breasts and cut them very thin

Coat both sides of each cutlet in flour

Dunk into egg, then into breadcrumbs

Heat up olive oil in a skillet or pan

Fry cutlets until crispy and golden brown

Drain on paper towels before serving
