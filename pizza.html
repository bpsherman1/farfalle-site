Pizza

This recipe was created through about a month of making pizza's everyday, and finding what worked best.

Our Signature Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

3 Hours before cooking take your dough out of the fridge

Place it in a big bowl after coating the bottom in olive oil

Place a towel or plastic wrap on top and let it rise for 3 hours

When ready heat oven to its hottest setting or preheat your pizza oven

Take your dough (cut to size) and roll it out to form a pizza

Spread olive oil over the dough

Take marinara sauce and gently pour and spread to about an inch of the edge of the dough to form a crust

Evenly spread out mozzarella cheese

Throw into oven and cook for 8-12 minutes
