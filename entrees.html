Entrees

Here are some of my go to meals, mixing all of the meats and sauces that I have made.

Pesto Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto
  • Chicken cutlet
  • Romano cheese

Instructions

Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente

While pasta cooks, slice cooked chicken cutlet into strips

Drain pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water

Toss hot pasta with pesto, adding pasta water as needed to create sauce

Top with chicken strips and freshly grated Romano cheese

Serve immediately

Meatball Marinara

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Marinara sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Reggiano parmigiano

Instructions

Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente

In separate pan, heat marinara sauce and add cooked meatballs

Simmer meatballs in sauce for 10-15 minutes to blend flavors

Drain pasta and toss with a small amount of sauce

Plate pasta, top with meatballs and extra sauce

Finish with freshly grated Reggiano parmigiano

Chicken Parmigiana

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Chicken cutlet
  • Reggiano parmigiano

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)

Place cooked chicken cutlets in baking dish

Top each cutlet with marinara sauce and sliced mozzarella

Bake for 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly

Remove from oven and sprinkle with Reggiano parmigiano

Serve with pasta or crusty bread
